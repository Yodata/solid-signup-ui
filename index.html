Why Solid?

The dominance of the World Wide Web by large corporate advertising engines has created data silos that are inaccessible to the people who generate that data -- people like you. All your pictures and posts, your profiles and networks -- they are largely out of your control.

Own Your Data

With a Solid account, your data is always under your control, because you decide where the data gets stored, who gets to see it and use it, and what applications get to have access to it.

Use Any App You Like

Solid data is separate from the applications that access and use it. If you build a photo library in one application, but a better one comes along, you can switch to that app without losing your data.

Let Data be Free

Free in the sense of freedom! You can decide what applications see your data. You set permissions and specify what is public, selectively shared, private, and secret!
